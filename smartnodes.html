● Smartnodes are similar to POS, and get rewards by locking a certain amount of coins in your own wallet. Currently 45% of coins will be rewarded to Smartnodes

● How to deploy nodes to get rewards?
1. If you do not have a VPS, you can deploy a virtual node, and you only need to lock a certain amount of coins.
https://github.com/Altcoin-Master/Bitfishcoin/tree/main/doc/smartnodes#b-wallet-settings-lock-the-coins-in-your-own-wallet

2. If you have an idle VPS (note that it is idle, because we don’t want people to buy VPS specifically for the node.) You can deploy a real node according to the following document, and don’t forget to open the 21002 port of the VPS.
https://github.com/Altcoin-Master/Bitfishcoin/tree/main/doc/smartnodes

3. As the bitfishcoin network matures Smartnode collateral ramps up and you will need to recreate your Smartnode in order to increase the collateral amount.
https://github.com/Altcoin-Master/Bitfishcoin/blob/main/doc/smartnodes/maintenance.md#increase-smartnode-collateral
